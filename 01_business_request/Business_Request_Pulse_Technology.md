# Business Request – Pulse Technology  

## Request Source
Sanitized stakeholder request documentation derived from an internal leadership inquiry.  
Included to demonstrate traceability from business question to analytical deliverables.

## Business Context
Company leadership is preparing for an upcoming organization-wide town hall and requires a data-driven walkthrough of order performance and customer behavior from **2019–2022**.

The objective is to understand historical performance, recent trends, and the effectiveness of key business initiatives in order to support strategic discussion and decision-making.

## Key Business Questions

- What were the overall sales trends during this period?
- What were the monthly and yearly growth rates?
- How is the loyalty program performing, and should it continue to be used?
- What were the refund rates and average order value over time?

## Expected Deliverables

- SQL-based analysis of revenue, orders, and customer behavior  
- Time-series trend visualization in Tableau  
- Executive summary highlighting insights, risks, and strategic recommendations  

## Definition of Success

Success is defined as:

- Clear visibility into **revenue and order trends from 2019–2022**
- Identification of **growth patterns, volatility, or decline**
- Evaluation of **loyalty program effectiveness**
- Actionable insights suitable for **executive-level presentation**
